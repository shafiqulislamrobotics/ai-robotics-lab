Join Our Lab

We welcome motivated researchers, students, and collaborators passionate about AI, Robotics, and real-world impact.

🎓

Graduate Students

PhD and MS students in AI, Robotics, Autonomous Systems, Computational Biology, or Climate Computing. Strong background in ML or control systems preferred.

🔬

Undergraduate Students

Motivated undergraduates interested in hands-on research with drones, mobile robots, self-driving cars, or AI/ML projects.

🤝

Industry Partners

We collaborate with industry on applied AI, robotics, and climate technology projects. Contact us to discuss partnership opportunities.

How to Apply

Send your CV/resume and a brief statement of research interests to Dr. Islam. Include relevant coursework, projects, or experience in AI, robotics, or engineering.
