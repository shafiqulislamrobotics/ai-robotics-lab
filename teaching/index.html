Teaching Courses

Online AI/ML courses from our labs, face-to-face XULA courses, and a 28-year legacy of engineering education.

Dr. Islam brings over 28 years of experience developing and teaching courses across Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Computer Science, Robotics and Mechatronics Engineering, Control Systems, and Autonomous Systems curricula.

He played a key role in developing the Bachelor of Science in Robotics and Mechatronics Engineering program at Xavier University of Louisiana (XULA) and has served as a founding member of several Robotics and Mechatronics Engineering programs internationally.

📧 Interested in any of these courses? Contact Dr. Islam at sislam3@xula.edu. Each course includes three projects directly from our latest research and development projects.

  • Support Vector Machines
  • Boosting, Bagging & ensemble methods
  • Clustering algorithms
  • Model evaluation & feature engineering
