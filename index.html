Welcome

Where AI Meets Real-World Engineering

Welcome to the AI for Robotics, Autonomous Systems, Computational Biology and Climate Computing research and development laboratory at Xavier University of Louisiana.

Our research focuses on developing AI-driven intelligent robotic systems, autonomous systems, and AI-driven computational biology and climate change prediction and prevention.

We combine advanced theory and real-world practical implementation to solve real-world challenges in robotics, autonomous systems, healthcare, bioinformatics, and climate change.
