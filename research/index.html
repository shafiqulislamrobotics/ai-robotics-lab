Research Areas

Advancing intelligent systems across four interconnected domains through AI, machine learning, and real-world deployment.

Area 01

AI for Robotics

Research on the development of intelligent robotic and autonomous systems capable of learning, adaptation, autonomous decision-making, and interaction with uncertain dynamic environments.

Research Topics

  • Machine learning and deep reinforcement learning for robot perception and navigation
  • Computer vision guided navigation, control and manipulations
  • LLM, RAG, and Agentic AI for human-robot interaction
  • Robot localization and mapping (SLAM)
  • Vision-based navigation and control
  • Intelligent mobile and aerial manipulation
  • Swarm intelligence, multi-robot coordination, control and manipulation
  • Human-robot collaboration
  • Haptics, virtual reality and tele-robotics over the internet
  • ML/DL for haptic-virtual reality based telemanipulation
Mobile Robotic Systems Platform
Industrial Robotic Systems
Autonomous Drone Fleet
Self-Driving Car Module
Area 02

Autonomous Systems

Development of autonomous systems capable of operating and manipulating aerial and mobile tasks safely and efficiently in dynamic and uncertain environments.

Research Topics

  • UAV/drone and mobile vehicle autonomy
  • Multi-agents (Drone/aerial, mobile/ground, industrial robot) coordination
  • Self-driving cars (small scale modules) autonomy
  • Swarm robotics (UAVs, Ground/mobile vehicles)
  • Swarm intelligence with Drone/UAV, Mobile vehicles and Euler Lagrange systems
  • Multi-agent systems and distributed optimization
  • Cyber-physical systems
  • Internet-based teleoperation, telemanipulation, and telepresence
Area 03

Computational Biology

Applying machine learning, deep reinforcement learning, LLM, and Agentic AI to biological, biomedical, and healthcare problems.

🔬

Biomedical Imaging

  • AI for medical imaging and diagnosis
  • Biomedical image processing
  • Disease detection and prevention
🧠

Neuroscience & Genomics

  • AI for computational neuroscience
  • Protein structure analysis
  • Genomics and bioinformatics
💊

Drug Discovery

  • Cancer drug delivery and efficacy
  • AI for bioinformatics
  • Transfer learning for biomedical applications
Area 04

Climate Computing & Environmental Intelligence

Applying data-driven AI for climate prediction and warning systems for the general public and decision makers, leveraging NASA's Global Climate Computing Networks at XULA.

Research Topics

  • Air quality monitoring & prediction (PM1–10, CO₂, CH₄, SO₂, NO₂)
  • Greenhouse gas prediction & climate analytics
  • Precipitation & flash flood forecasting
  • Climate computing & Earth observation
  • Autonomous environmental sensing
  • Environmental digital twins & decision intelligence
  • Explainable AI for climate resilience
